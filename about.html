About us




“Need food and a good place to eat? Welcome to our humble place where you can eat good food peacefully.” Customer Satisfaction is what we judge ourselves by. If they are delighted, we have served our purpose of 'being'.

Little touches like a live kitchen serving freshly cooked food and other international variants of almost Best foods of the North Indian and South Indian and also Chinese foods like(Noodles,Manchurian) besides warm your stomach with our traditional touch, Nidhi'S Restaurant becomes the perfect place to stop the world a bit - and indulge your senses to have the best time with complete flavourness.

Nidhi's Restaurant is a family restaurant loved by one and all - school going kids, teenagers, friends, executives, and the elderly. One has to admit though, this idea of reaching out to everyone was naturally borrowed from Nidhi's Time - it is after all, the meeting point of many different generations in almost all Indian families.

Just another opportunity for healing. The food of peace, of satisfaction. As people slowly shift to organic and healthier versions of food, we too are looking in that direction. Our confidence in our intentions, offerings and service has not been misplaced - Nidhi's Restaurant attracts customers throughout the week - as much as it does on weekends. With a food that literally prides itself to serve happily, Nidhi's Restaurant is almost like a family experience to all those associated with it. We are looking forward for you to join us.
